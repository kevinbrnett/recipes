Classic Quesadilla

Description

If done properly a plain quesadilla with require no dipping sauces. If you disagree you don't know how to make a good quesadilla. It's just facts so buckle up Betsy cause we're rollin' into FLAVOR TOWN.

Ingredients

  • CORN tortillas
  • Shredded colby jack cheese
  • Shredded pepper jack cheese
  • love

It's Cookin' Time

  1. Before we start cooking read this:
    • You need to buy a block of cheese and shred it yourself. If you plan on buying pre-shredded cheese don't bother with the recipe. Who are we kidding? you could use the exercise and this is the KEY FACTOR in acheiving absolutley busting flavor.
    • It is essential to use corn tortillas. If you are going to use a flour tortilla, don't. You don't even deserve a quesadilla. Go to Taco Bell and never come back.
  2. Now that you are prepared to create art, let's begin!
  3. Heat a skillet on high until rrrripping hawt.
  4. Put desired amount of tortillas onto the skillet. Heat one side for 30 seconds or until pliable. Then flip the tortillas.
  5. The exact ratio of cheeses is up to personal taste. I usually do 2/3 colby jack and 1/3 pepper jack. (pro tip: I find that Tillamook melts the best)
  6. Sprinkle cheese to cover each tortilla. You can never have too much so really go nuts and knock yourself out. deserve it King/Queen/Jester/Witcher. (I think that covers everything?)
  7. Now fold each tortilla in half and continue to cook until chrispy. Flip and finish cooking until chrispy.
